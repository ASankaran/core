With over 1000 attendees and 50 open source mentors in 2019, the hackathon has grown to become one of the largest and most well-regarded in the nation. This year, we're proud to welcome you to the 7th annual HackIllinois from February 28 - March 1, 2020, on the Urbana-Champaign campus. For more information, please visit the website: https://hackillinois.org.
